Wager — Story Brand Soundbite Strategy

Unified Angle

It's hard to keep your friends accountable for dares and bets without awkwardness or forgetting who owes what.

Make a bet. Prove it. Let your friends decide.

Enlightenment Collateral (Front Porch)

1. Guide: "The 7-Day Accountability Challenge"

7 daily dares to do with friends — each with a pre-made Wager template:

  1. Run 1km — fastest time wins
  2. Best coffee art photo
  3. No phone after 9pm
  4. Cook dinner from scratch
  5. 100 pushups throughout the day
  6. Best sunset photo
  7. Something you've been putting off

2. Use Case Collection: "Real Wagers You Can Steal"

  • Run 5k under 25min or buy dinner
  • Best homemade pizza photo — winner gets bragging rights
  • Read 3 books this month or donate to charity
  • No social media for a week
  • Learn to juggle in 7 days
  • Master a song on guitar
  • Who can save the most money this month?

3. Screenshot Walkthrough: "How to Settle a Bet in 60 Seconds"

  1. Alice creates a wager → shares invite link
  2. Bob accepts → runs the bet
  3. Bob submits proof (Strava link, photo)
  4. Alice votes → wager resolves automatically
  5. Both see the outcome — no arguments
